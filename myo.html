Diagnosing Foliage Problems

Here is a quick guide to diagnosing common problems with plant foliage:
  1. Yellow
    • Wet soil
    • Low fertility
    • Root rot pathogens
    • Roots cut or damage
    1. Gophers
    2. Moles
    3. Insects
  2. Necrotic spots
  3. White powder
  4. Light and dark green areas
  5. Holes or chewed areas
